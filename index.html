THE NAME

LAMINE
YAMAL.

“Lamine Yamal” is his given name; Nasraoui Ebana are his surnames. Reports say the given names honour people who helped his family before he was born.

NASRAOUI · EBANA
01

OFF THE PITCH

Friends and teammates describe a relaxed, funny, family-minded competitor. In his downtime, he is known to enjoy music, video games and being around the squad — while keeping much of his personal life private.

02

THE CIRCLE

His Barcelona and Spain generation includes Pau Cubarsí, Pedri, Gavi, Alejandro Balde and Nico Williams. Their shared confidence is part of the new-era energy around club and country.
